Why it's strong

High confidence, low noise — an alert needs evidence from ≥2 independent systems, so the SOC gets signal, not fatigue.
Nothing slips through — every new IP/user/URL reviewed from its first request; 17 detections + a daily AI sweep for subtle/novel attacks.
Cost-efficient — routine detection is plain queries (no AI, ~zero cost, 48×/day); Opus used only on a confirmed HIGH and once daily.
